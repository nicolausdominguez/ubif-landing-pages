How do I know I need a new watch battery?

Watch Has Stopped

A watch that has stopped likely has a dead battery. Watch batteries should be replaced every 1 to 3 years to make sure they keep accurate time.

Second Hand Jumping Forward

Some watches will notify you if the battery is low by causing the second hand to jump forward, usually in 5 second intervals.

Watch Keeping Incorrect Time

Most modern, especially quartz, watches are known for keeping highly accurate and almost perfect time. If you notice that your watch is not keeping accurate time, your battery may need to be replaced.
