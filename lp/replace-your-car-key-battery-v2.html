How do I know I need a new key battery?

Key fob has a short range

If you notice that you have to be close to your vehicle before your fob will unlock the doors or pop the trunk, you might need a new battery.

Unlock or start buttons NOT WORKING

If you notice that you have to push function buttons more than once before you get a response, you might need a new battery.

Key Battery Low indicator

If your car tells you that your key battery is low, you definitely need a new battery.
